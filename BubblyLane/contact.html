Fun Industry Fact

According to Beverage Industry (July 11, 2012), Millennials are driving adult beverage industry trends. Consumers aged 21-24 are more likely than average to consume wine, and are more likely to do so with high frequency.

When 2015 ends, the entire Millennial generation, 77.7 million, will be of legal drinking age, representing a major population bump up from its predecessor, Gen X, which has only 49.7 million members. This is a plus for the category, which may see growth down the line, given that this group has the most years of buying ahead of them. Inspiring consumer loyalty and growing alongside these consumers as they age should be prioritized. Products can be positioned as new and interesting for this consumer group but also instill a sense of identity and trustworthiness that will encourage repeated use.


Inspiration Behind BubblyLane

Wandering through the wine aisle one day and frustrated by the hundreds of choices with no clear way on identifying what to choose, a simple inspiration struck us. Why not have an iPad on the shelf that guides consumers to their perfect wine match? With no solutions in the market like this, we sought out to create one. WinePad and BrewPad are iPad apps to help your customers select the right wine and craft beer for them while they're in the aisle. Contact us for a demo and more information.
