Inspiration Behind BubblyLane

Wandering through the wine aisle one day and frustrated by the hundreds of choices with no clear way on identifying what to choose, a simple inspiration struck us. Why not have an iPad on the shelf that guides consumers to their perfect wine match? With no solutions in the market like this, we sought out to create one. WinePad and BrewPad are iPad apps to help your customers select the right wine and craft beer for them while they're in the aisle. Contact us for a demo and more info
